MY name is Allen and This is my personal Blog


I'm Ekejiuba Allen Ugochukwu — a writer, thinker, and builder based in Nigeria. I use this blog as a space to work through ideas that don't fit neatly into a tweet or a deck. My interests stretch across technology, culture, African identity, and the craft of building things that matter. I write when something genuinely moves me to — which means posts here are infrequent but considered. When I'm not writing, I'm probably reading, in conversation with someone interesting, or quietly working on something new.
